Hello — I’m Aziz

Undergraduate researcher exploring AI × Clinical Psychology, working-memory modeling (fMRI), and neural MSA.

I’m a B.Sc. student in Psychology at McGill University (Bashivan Lab). I build transformer-based models for cognitive tasks and DNA multiple sequence alignment, and I’m excited about aligning model representations with human brain activity. Long‑term, I aim to bridge computational psychiatry with reliable, deployable ML tools.
